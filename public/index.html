11th August 2013 — Sebastopol, CA

Our Story

Ryan ♥ Lisa

We met our freshmen year at UCLA”s AACF. In groups, went to see musicians like Jars of Clay and Jason Mraz, went to Ashland to for a Shakespearean festival. We didn’t really keep in touch while I was in New York City for grad school and work. When I moved back to L.A., I was still looking for work, but got tickets to see The Dave Matthews Band at the summer series for The Ellen DeGeneres Show. I remembered that Ryan loved DMB and had a flexible schedule.

With that short experience, it led to two other show visits, and we started hanging out with other UCLA friends who were still in the area. Throughout this time, we were just hanging out like we were in college. There was an opportunity for us to go to Kenya through Heavenly Treasures and his church. We had an amazing experience there, but Ryan avoided me like the plague. It wasn’t until a month after we got back that Ryan called me and asked me to dinner. To be clear, he told me upfront that it was a date. It took a while for me to catch up, but after 2 years, he popped the question and we’re looking at spending many more years together! Hurray!
